02

The Problem

Well-Being Programs Don't Reach
the People Who Need Them Most

Low EAP Utilization

In Asia, EAP usage averages 2-5% vs. 8-10% in the US. Cultural stigma and lack of awareness keep employees from self-referring.

Shift Workers Excluded

Lunch-and-learn sessions, wellness fairs, and office yoga don't reach DC operators, factory workers, or night-shift employees.

One-Size-Fits-All

Programs designed in HQ don't account for timezone differences, cultural norms, or individual work rhythms across 10+ APAC countries.

"We need to build well-being into the flow of work, not add it as one more thing on the to-do list."
03

The Solution

AI-Powered Nudges That Adapt to Each Employee

KeepWell AI delivers micro-interventions based on behavioral science, personalized to each person's schedule, location, and language.

How It Works

⏰ Detects time of day and work phase (circadian + ultradian rhythm)
🌤️ Checks live weather for context-appropriate suggestions
🎯 Selects from 8 dimensions, avoiding repetition
📎 Delivers a nudge with 1-line science backing
📊 Tracks engagement and flags burnout risk

Example Nudges

🏃 Physical (2:30 PM, sunny)
"It's 26°C and clear outside. Step out for a 5-min walk."
📎 Outdoor walking doubles recovery benefit (Kaplan, 1995)

💭 Emotional (4:00 PM)
"Name one emotion you're feeling right now."
📎 Affect labeling reduces amygdala activity by 43% (Lieberman, 2007)

🌙 Late Night (11:30 PM)
"Sleep debt can't be repaid on weekends. Save your work."
📎 Walker, 2017

04

Framework

8 Dimensions of Wellness (SAMHSA)

Not just "drink water" reminders. A holistic model covering all aspects of human well-being.

🏃 Physical

Movement, posture, hydration, sleep

💭 Emotional

Self-awareness, stress management, affect labeling

💼 Occupational

Boundaries, focus, purpose, workload

🤝 Social

Connection, belonging, gratitude

📖 Intellectual

Learning, curiosity, growth mindset

🌿 Environmental

Workspace, nature, air quality

💰 Financial

Benefits awareness, spending habits

✨ Spiritual

Purpose, values, gratitude, meaning

Each dimension has 5-8 evidence-based tips with peer-reviewed citations. Total: 88 nudges (EN + 繁中).
